What is Meditation?

Meditation comes in various forms; only being defined as deep contemplation focused on moving the mind into a restful and relaxed state, and avoiding stressful, depressed, or anxious environments/situations. While most traditional forms of meditation revolve around peaceful naturesque music, beautiful nature pictures, and yoga exercises, meditation truly extends to anything that will allow you peace of mind; thus reaching mental outlets such as art, dance, exercise, and playing an instrument.
